Privacy Policy

How Lumina Scan handles user information.

This policy explains the categories of information we collect, why we use it, and how users can contact us about privacy concerns.

Information we may collect

  • Account details such as name and email address
  • Profile information users choose to provide
  • Payment-related references needed to verify premium access
  • App usage information needed for security, support, and performance review
  • Document metadata needed to operate scan history and export flows

How information is used

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To process and verify premium unlock payments
  • To support password reset, login protection, and account recovery
  • To improve app performance, reliability, and support response
  • To comply with legal obligations where applicable

Sharing and service providers

Lumina Scan may rely on third-party infrastructure and payment services to operate core functions such as authentication, payment verification, and monitoring. Data is shared only to the extent reasonably necessary to deliver those services.

Data security

We aim to use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to reduce unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. No system can be guaranteed perfectly secure, so users should also protect access to their devices and accounts.

User choices

  • Users may contact support regarding account or privacy concerns
  • Users may request help regarding profile information corrections
  • Users may stop using the app at any time
