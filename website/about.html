About Us

Designed for document workflows that feel lighter and faster.

Lumina Scan is a mobile document utility focused on practical everyday work: capturing pages, improving readability, signing PDFs, exporting clean files, and keeping scan history manageable.

Our purpose

We built Lumina Scan for people who want a focused scanning experience without a cluttered interface. The app combines document capture, enhancement, signing, and export into a single flow that is easy to understand on mobile.

What the app offers

  • Camera and gallery-based document import
  • Page review and enhancement tools
  • E-sign support for signed PDF workflows
  • Export to PDF, JPG, PNG, and TXT
  • Account-backed access and profile management
  • One-time premium unlock for expanded usage limits

How we work

We aim to keep pricing simple, support communication direct, and product decisions centered on document quality, export usefulness, and dependable user access.
